How to wash your hands properly

Apply soap on hand

Wet hands, apply soap covering all surfaces. Soap lifts dirt and microbes, facilitating rinsing.

Learn More

Palm to Palm

Rub hands together, creating lather. Scrub palms, fingers, and spaces between fingers.

Learn More

Between Fingers

Interlock fingers, rub spaces between them. Clean areas around nails for comprehensive disinfection.

Learn More

Back of the hands

Rotate hands, rub backs against opposite palm. Clean backs, wrists, and forearms.

Learn More

Clean with water

Rinse hands with running water, removing soap residue, germs, and dirt for cleanliness.

Learn More

Use Towel to dry

Dry hands using clean towel or air dry. Eliminate moisture to prevent germ buildup.
