What are the main Symptoms?

Common symptoms of COVID-19 include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. In severe cases, the virus can lead to pneumonia, severe acute respiratory syndrome, kidney failure, and even death. It's crucial to recognize these symptoms and seek medical attention if you experience them

  • Fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Shortness of Breath
